About Me

I have a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Earth Science and I recently finished a Master's degree in Applied Data Science. Professionally, I've worked on projects related to population characteristics, urban dynamics, climate change, and critical infrastructure. I am passionate about applying my GIS and data science skills to address real-world challenges and opportunities in various domains and industries. I love to learn and explore data.
